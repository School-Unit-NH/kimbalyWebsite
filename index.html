About Us

A Strong Foundation for Every Child

At Kimberly Primary School, we blend excellent teaching with good values. Our classrooms are interactive, structured, and child-friendly—so children enjoy learning while building real academic skills.

We focus on reading, writing, mathematics, and science, while also developing confidence through arts, sports, music, and practical life skills. Parents are our partners, and we keep communication open.
